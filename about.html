About Me

Hi! I’m Sinthya — a UX & Product Design student who loves creating simple, intuitive, and beautiful digital experiences. I’m passionate about designing interfaces that help people feel comfortable and confident while navigating digital products.

I’m still early in my professional journey, but I love learning how thoughtful design choices can shape how people interact with technology. My interests include user flows, product thinking, and creating clean, friendly layouts.

Outside of design, I run Decor Blossom, my small creative business where I make custom bouquets, ribbon pieces, decorative bottles, and event decor. Working with color, texture, and composition in my décor work inspires my design eye and helps me bring warmth and personality into my UX projects.

I’m excited to grow my skills and build a portfolio of meaningful, user-centered projects. Thanks for stopping by!
