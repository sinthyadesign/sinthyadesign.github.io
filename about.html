About Me

Hi! I’m Sinthya — a UX & Product Design student who loves creating soft, intuitive, and meaningful digital experiences. I’m passionate about clear layouts, emotional design, and problem-solving through creativity.

Beyond school, I run Decor Blossom, where I design custom bouquets, ribbon arrangements, and decorative bottle pieces. Working with colour, balance, and composition in my floral business inspires my approach to digital design.

I’m building a portfolio that blends aesthetics, usability, and storytelling. My goal is to grow into a product designer who creates experiences that make people feel guided, confident, and understood.
